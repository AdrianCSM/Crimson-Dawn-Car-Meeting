Crimson Dawn

Car Meeting

2022

An amazing car meeting during the best sunsets in the whole world. This is the best place to show your tuning, mods and take pictures. It will take place in Japan, Tokyo, during the "Red Sun" sunsets.

2022.08.18(Thu) ~ 21(Sun)

@ Akihabara UDX parkade - lower levels.

Main program

  • Presenting

    Every participant has the oportunity to present their car and show his mods. This will happen only on day 1.

  • Sound Test

    This is the time when everyone will show the car's whine and sutututu. Advised to take protection.

  • Drifting

    We will move our location so the cars can drift around, doing donuts and everything else.

  • Contest

    We will gather votes, and the person that has the most, will get an Akarprovic Exhaust for his car.

  • The Reveal

    We will reveal the winner. The other contenstants will get something as well, but it's a surprize.
